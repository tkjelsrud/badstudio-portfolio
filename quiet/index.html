About notoms

notoms is the creative alias of Thomas Kjelsrud, a restless soul navigating the balance between fatherhood, a full-time software engineering career, and his artistic pursuits. Music is his outlet, a nightly practice driven by internal restlessness, anxiety, and occasional moments of joy.

His sound circles around ambient, eerie themes, often built on synths and samplers, while weaving in guitar, bass, and his distinct vocal style, which sits somewhere between singing and spoken word. Though notoms occasionally plays light gigs and collaborates with bands, his true focus is in solo production, where he creates music meant to provoke thought and introspection. His work blends atmospheric textures that are both soothing and unsettling, providing a soundscape for contemplation and wonder. Always abstract, yet with a subtle drive, notoms invites listeners into a world where the eerie and the calming coexist.
