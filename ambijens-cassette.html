Ambijens / Digital / Cassette Edition

"Ambijens” is a restrained ambient EP released via Badstudio.no, capturing a period of immersion in drones, frozen reverbs and slow-moving synth textures. Built in Ableton and shaped through long sessions of listening rather than performance, the four tracks move between cold, static ambience and more rhythmic, cinematic moments. Released digitally in 2022, with a limited cassette edition following in 2023, “Ambijens” documents a phase rather than a destination — a condensed snapshot of where the sound was at the time.
