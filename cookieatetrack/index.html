Cookie Ate Track - lofi sampling on Octatrack

Cookie Ate Track is a concept for live performance by Thomas Kjelsrud. It's all about raw quirky beats and floating soundscapes; accessible, unpolished. Inspired by music from 90'ties and early 2000; the do-it-yourself days when your web page was flashing under construction.

Old school lofi beats dubby spacy housy techy idm ambient querky and all-sorts. All Octatrack hardware sampler, cassette tape loops and synths.
